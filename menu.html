Our Menu

Discover our delicious offerings that will surely make your mouth water!

Veg-Momo

Delight in our Veg Momo, a popular Nepali delicacy featuring steamed dumplings filled with a savory mix of finely chopped vegetables and aromatic spices. Each bite offers a burst of flavor, wrapped in a tender, delicately steamed dough. Perfectly paired with a spicy dipping sauce, these momos are a must-try for anyone seeking an authentic taste of Nepal.

Masala Dosa

A crispy, golden-brown crepe made from fermented rice and lentil batter, filled with a savory spiced potato mixture. Served with coconut chutney and tangy sambar, Masala Dosa is a beloved South Indian classic that combines rich flavors and delightful textures in every bite.

Pani Puri

Pani Puri features crisp, hollow puris filled with a spicy mix of tamarind water, tangy chutneys, and a medley of flavors including chickpeas, potatoes, and onions. Served in bite-sized portions, each puri bursts with a delightful blend of tangy, spicy, and savory tastes.
